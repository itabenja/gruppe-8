Algeria's Renewable Energy Situation

Algeria has a low percentage of renewable energy usage, primarily due to its reliance on fossil fuels. Government policies and investments in green energy are still in the early stages.

Iceland's Renewable Energy Success

Iceland has one of the highest shares of renewable energy in the world, mainly due to its abundant geothermal and hydroelectric power resources.
